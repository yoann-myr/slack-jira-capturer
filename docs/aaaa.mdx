# Release Notes — February 2026

**Version 2026.2**

---

## Highlights

This release introduces the new Insights page, providing clear analysis across climate, signals, and ventilation. It is powered by our evolving Data Platform, which we have enhanced over the past few months to deliver reliable, well-structured data. The release also brings increased reliability, performance, and data accuracy throughout the system.

---

## MyPortal — Customers

**Performance & display**

- Faster, more responsive views for large datasets — especially Zone Assets in buildings with areas greater than 10,000 m².
- Improved handling and display of sensor values, with clearer units and error messages.

**Insights — Climate**

- Historical climate data (temperature, CO₂, relative humidity) with up to one year of history.
- Climate thresholds displayed to help assess optimization performance.

**Insights — R/W**

- Pinpoint deviations that require attention to ensure optimal energy savings results.
- Toggle between Office hours and All hours.

**Insights — CO2 Detailed**

- Overview of CO2 levels throughout the day and week.
- Suggestions for adjusting ventilation start/stop times based on CO2 level analysis.

> **Note:** The Insights pages are still in development — more views will be added in upcoming releases.

---

## BackOffice & Integration — Partners

- **Yggio** — Added as a building data provider to support seamless integration.
- Dataset Viewer now shows clear indicators for frozen data periods, making faulty data easier to spot.
- Zone Assets building integration tools improved, including enhancements to the Components Editor.

---

## Control & Optimization

- Greater robustness in optimization and training workflows, with improved handling of data issues — resulting in higher uptime and more consistent savings.
- Improved handling of non-office periods, especially around end-of-year holidays when many systems are offline. Optimization now continues reliably during these periods instead of failing on missing data.
